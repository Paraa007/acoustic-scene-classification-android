App Icon Explorations

Ten candidates for FZI Acoustic Scene Classification.

Each tile shows the icon inside Android's adaptive squircle (192×192) and its round-mask preview, so both clipping shapes can be judged side-by-side. The palette is intentionally muted — sage, slate, dusty teal, olive, mocha, bone — to keep the icon legible on busy launcher backgrounds without leaning on neon or glow.

Direction

  • ThemeAcoustic / audio classification — waveforms, bars, spectrograms, mic, ear, sound radiation.
  • PaletteSage, slate, dusty teal, olive, charcoal, bone, mocha. No bright accents, no glow.
  • ToneFZI research-institute calm — clean geometry, restrained contrast, mono-friendly.
  • FormatInline SVG, hand-drawn paths, fixed 192×192 viewport, safe-zone respected for round-mask.
