FZI KARLSRUHE

Five muted dark variants. Three rounded, two sharp.

A second pass at colour. The accent stays calm, the surface stays quiet. Two of them drop every radius so you can see the same screens with sharp corners. Pick the one that reads best.

Brief

  • ColourMuted only. No glowing accents, no high-saturation reds, no neon greens. Each scene-class hue is a desaturated version of the v2 palette.
  • CornersVariants 1-3 keep the v2 rounded tiles. Variants 4-5 zero every radius (tiles, buttons, pills, chips, status icons).
  • TypeSame as v2 — Hanken Grotesk for UI, IBM Plex Mono for labels and numbers.
  • Content14 mocked Android screens per variant, identical to v2. Only colour and corner-radius differ.
